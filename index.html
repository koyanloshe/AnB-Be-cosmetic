London's best Cosmetic Surgery Brand Now in Kenya

Be Cosmetic Clinics is a highly reputed and well established brand in the UK. Having noticed a demand for cosmetic surgery here in Kenya; Be has now ventured forth with its very first clinic in the heart of Nairobi to deliver top notch surgical procedures under the aegis of its Founder and Chief Surgeon Dr. Hassan Nurein. With its dedication to quality care, its passion for empowering the lives of people through realistic results and it's professional attitude, Be aims to bring world-class training, know-how and service to the people of Kenya.
